EZ-WebCraft

I am a linux engineer by day and coder by night. I am an ambitious problem solver with a passion for programming, Unix/Linux and web technology as a whole. I’m continually to evaluating and upgrading my skills, so I can stay at the cutting edge of technology.

I have experience with variety of web technology. I also believe in design which less equals more! some the web front-end technology proficient is HTML/CSS,javascript/JQuery.

As a linux engineer by day, I have work with many Linux/Unix Environment from Open-source: Ubuntu,Fedora,Debian and FreeBSD to commercial OS like AIX, Solarizes and RedHat. Also, I feel at home with the terminal and many of their utilities and scripting technology.
